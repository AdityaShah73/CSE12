    4. ABOUT UNIVERSITY

    Description List

    ABES Engineering College is a private engineering college located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was established in 2000 and is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU). The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, including Computer Science, Electronics and Communication, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Information Technology.
